## 七、可能遇到的质疑

### Q: "实盘验证不足"

回应:
```
We acknowledge the limitation of backtest-only validation.
However, our system has been validated on 6 months of A-share
data with realistic constraints (transaction costs, slippage).
Future work will include paper trading validation.

Importantly, our focus is on the methodology (multi-scale
temporal reasoning + dynamic weighting) which is validated
through rigorous backtesting.
```

### Q: "模型过于复杂"

回应:
```
While our system combines multiple models, each component has
a specific role:
- CTM: Multi-scale temporal reasoning (necessary for factor decay)
- Mamba: Long-range pattern detection (efficient alternative to Transformer)
- Dynamic weights: Market regime adaptation (essential for robustness)

The 7-layer architecture is designed for complementary strengths,
not redundancy. Experiments show each layer contributes positively.
```
